# Defined for [[Second-Order Analysis]] Circuits as:
$$x''(t)+2\alpha x'(t)+\omega_0^2x(t)=0$$
## For Series LRC:
$$\omega_0=\frac{1}{\sqrt{LC}}$$
$$\alpha=\zeta\omega_0=\frac{R}{2L}$$
## For Parallel LRC:
$$\omega_0=\frac{1}{\sqrt{LC}}$$
$$\alpha=\zeta\omega_0=\frac{1}{2RC}$$
Over Damped When:
$$\alpha>\omega_0$$
Critically Damped When:
$$\alpha=\omega_0$$
Under Damped When:
$$\alpha<\omega_0$$
See [[AC-Steady State]].
