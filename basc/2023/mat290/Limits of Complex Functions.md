## Conditions for the existence of $\lim\limits_{z\to z_0}f(z)=L$
- $f(z)$ is defined in a [[Neighbourhood]] of $z_0$
- if for some $\epsilon > 0 \space\exists\space\delta>0\space\text{st:}$$$0<|f(z)-L|<\epsilon\text{ and }0<|z_0-z|<\delta$$
