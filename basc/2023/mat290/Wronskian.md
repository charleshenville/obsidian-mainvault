## Of a set of functions with:
$$c_1f_1(x)+c_2f_2(x)+...+c_nf_n(x)=0$$
$$W(f_1+f_2+...+f_n)=\det(
\begin{bmatrix}
    f_{1}& f_{2} & f_{3} & \dots & f_{n} \\
    f'_{1}& f'_{2} & f'_{3} & \dots & f'_{n} \\
    \vdots&\vdots&\vdots&\ddots&\vdots \\
    f^{(n)}_{1}& f^{(n)}_{2} & f^{(n)}_{3} & \dots & f^{(n)}_{n} 
\end{bmatrix})
$$
