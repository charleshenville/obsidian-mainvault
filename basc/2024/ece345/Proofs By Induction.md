## Goal is to prove a predicate true
- Based on the "Well-Ordered" principle of non-negative integers
$$P(1)\cap\forall n\{P(n)\to P(n+1)\}\to\forall n,P(n)$$
- Where $P(1)$ is referred to as a basis that is known to be true
